When I made risotto for the first time

When I learned how to cook, I told my teacher I wanted to make risotto. At the time, I was told this was a very complicated dish and it was not something I should try right away. I was somewhat defiant and decided to learn how to make this dish. My first risotto recipe was a simple risotto side dish and I am here to share that this is not as complicated as I was initially told. For those of you who haven’t tried to make this dish, thinking it was too advanced, I am here to let you know, this is something everyone can make, so long as you are willing to take the time to create this delicious dish. I am no chef, I took cooking in high school as one of my electives and I enjoyed trying and tasting new recipes. I want to share a few recipes I found online and some of the different ingredients I switched up to personalize the dish. I hope this will inspire you to try these recipes and possibly others.
